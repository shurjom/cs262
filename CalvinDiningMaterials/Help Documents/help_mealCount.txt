Section 2: Meal Count

You can use the meal count feature of the Calvin Dining Hall app to keep track of the number of meals you have left and add/deduct meals.

1. Pull out the sliding menu on the left side of the app.
2. Tap on the meal count option.
3. Enter number of meals you desire to track using the popup keyboard. 
4. Add or deduct the meals you desire every time one is used.(The meals left will be automatically stored and be available the next time you use the app.)
