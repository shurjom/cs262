Logging in to the Calvin Dining app:

You can log into the Calvin Dining app to save your meal count.

If you have not logged in before:
1.	From the Log In screen, select “New User”.
2.	Choose a Username and enter it into the Username field.
3.	Confirm your Username by entering it into the Confirm Username field.
4.	Choose a Password and enter it into the Password field.
5.	Confirm your Password by entering it into the Confirm Password field.

If you are a returning user:
1.	Enter your Username and Password into the fields provided.
